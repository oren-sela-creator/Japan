Your base in the heart of Namba. Head out early — Kyoto is ~30 min by Shinkansen or ~75 min by Kintetsu limited express from Osaka-Namba Station.
01
Nature

Arashiyama Bamboo Forest

嵐山竹林の小径

⏰ Open 24 hours

4.3 · 21k reviews

A cathedral of towering stalks — arrive before 8 am for near-solitude. The bamboo sways and hushes the entire city behind you.
02
Temple

Daigo-ji

醍醐寺

⏰ 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM

4.4 · 5.6k reviews

A UNESCO complex with a five-story pagoda and mossy stone paths. The pond reflects maple trees in autumn like a painted scroll.
03
Temple

Benten-dō

弁天堂 · 醍醐寺

⏰ Within Daigo-ji grounds

4.4 · 128 reviews

The iconic island pavilion hidden among waterfalls and a looping forest path. Perfectly serene in the early morning before crowds arrive.
04
Shrine

Fushimi Inari Taisha

伏見稲荷大社

⏰ Open 24 hours

4.6 · 85k reviews

Thousands of vermillion torii gates spiraling up the mountain. Go at dusk or late evening for a completely different, haunting atmosphere.
05 · Lunch
Ramen

Kyoto Engine Ramen

京都エンジンラーメン

⏰ 12:00 PM – 10:30 PM

4.5 · 3.8k reviews

Rich, deep-flavored broth with vegan and gluten-free options. Located in a covered arcade district — popular for very good reason.

⏰ 11:30 AM – 10:00 PM · closed Wed

Authentic Neapolitan pizza in a cozy Kyoto corner. Fresh pasta made daily; the steak earns particular praise from regulars.

eggslut 京都四条店

Brioche egg sandwiches and crispy hash browns across three spacious floors. A rare quality Western brunch stop in central Kyoto.

Day 02 · East Kyoto · Higashiyama Walk

Pagodas, Incense & Matcha↗

Tap title to open full route in Google Maps · 6 stops · transit + walking

Start · Hotel
Base

Hotel Keihan Namba Grande

ホテル京阪 なんばグランデ

⌂ Namba, Osaka

4.4 · 704 reviews

Head to Kyoto again — today is a slower, more meditative day along the Higashiyama ridge. Take the train to Keage or Higashiyama Station.
01
Temple

Nanzen-ji

南禅寺

⏰ 8:40 AM – 4:30 PM

4.5 · 12k reviews

A great Zen complex at the foot of the Higashiyama mountains. The brick Meiji-era aqueduct threading through ancient temple grounds is strangely beautiful.
02
Temple

Kiyomizu-dera Temple

清水寺

⏰ 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

4.6 · 67k reviews

UNESCO-listed. A wooden stage built without nails over the hillside — sweeping views of Kyoto spread endlessly below. Visit at opening for silence.
03
Pagoda

Hōkan-ji Temple

法観寺 · 八坂の塔

⏰ Interior Sat–Sun · always visible outside

4.6 · 5.4k reviews

The five-story pagoda that defines Kyoto's skyline. The narrow stone lane below at sunset is one of Japan's most-photographed streets.
04
Temple

Yasaka Kōshin-dō

八坂庚申堂

⏰ 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

4.3 · 2.6k reviews

Hundreds of round tied cloth balls covering every surface in vivid color. A burst of joy hidden in Higashiyama's quiet lanes — don't skip it.
05 · Coffee
Café

Starbucks Ninenzaka

スターバックス 京都二寧坂ヤサカ茶屋店

⏰ 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM

4.4 · 6k reviews

A 100-year-old machiya townhouse with tatami seating and no Starbucks sign on the door. Widely regarded as the most beautiful Starbucks in the world.
06 · Finish
Matcha Café

MACCHA HOUSE

抹茶館 京都祇園四条

⏰ 11:00 AM – 8:00 PM

4.1 · 116 reviews

End the day with a matcha tiramisu made from high-grade Uji matcha. Silky cream, real bitterness. A perfect Kyoto farewell.
